Story

Our Love Story

First Meet

Our story began at a bus station, where fate intervened. After heartfelt phone conversations, I gave Annick a ride from Remera to Kanombe. That day marked the start of a beautiful journey filled with love and shared dreams.

First Date

On our enchanting first date at Nyandungu EcoPark, the setting sun painted the sky in gold and pink hues. Under nature's embrace, we revealed our pasts and dreams, discovering a destined connection. Amidst leafy whispers and bird songs, we decided to embark on a shared journey, bound by love's promise. This evening forever holds our hearts.

Proposal

At a good stage of our commitment, a momentous decision hung in the air. I was on the brink of departing for the United States, the final leg of my Masters studies. In that tender evening light, as we journeyed towards her home, I knew what needed to be said. Seated side by side, the world outside a blur, I turned to Annick with a heart brimming with love and conviction. I made a vow, one that would bind our futures together. "When I return," I promised, "I will make you mine forever." Under the canvas of an evening sky, with the weight of promise and hope, we forged a bond that transcended borders and time zones.

Engagement

On a radiant Sunday morning, the very place where our love story began witnessed a new chapter unfold. With the support and presence of dear friends, we orchestrated a moment that will forever be etched in our hearts. Gathered at the sacred grounds of Nyandungu EcoPark, our love story officially blossomed into something extraordinary. It was here that we embarked on the journey of a lifetime, bound by the promise of forever. As the sun painted the sky with hues of gold, we stood hand in hand, ready to embrace this new phase of our lives. Surrounded by the echoes of our beginnings, we knew that this day marked the beginning of a love that would continue to flourish and spread its wings.
